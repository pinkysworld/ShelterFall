About the Game

Shelter Fall is a post-apocalyptic survival strategy game where players manage a small group of survivors, build and upgrade a shelter, research technologies, and defend against nightly waves of hostile creatures. Each run is a tense 20-day roguelike journey where every decision determines whether your shelter stands or falls.

Key Features

  • Roguelike 20-day survival campaigns with permadeath tension
  • Real-time night defense scenes powered by SpriteKit
  • 10 buildable structures with unique strategic benefits
  • 5 defense types, each upgradeable to level 5
  • 25 researchable technologies in a deep tech tree
  • 30+ narrative events with meaningful choices
  • 4 difficulty levels: Easy, Normal, Hard, Apocalypse
  • 14 achievements and meta-progression between runs
  • Procedural map exploration and survivor management
  • Original sound design and music
  • No paywalls — fully playable for free
